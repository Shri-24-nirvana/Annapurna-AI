Our core AI model analyzes past consumption patterns, student feedback, and external factors like weather to provide precise cooking quantities for every meal. This eliminates guesswork and drastically reduces overproduction.

Based on demand forecasts, the platform helps you order the right amount of raw materials. Prevent overstocking of perishable goods and reduce inventory holding costs, ensuring freshness and minimizing spoilage.
